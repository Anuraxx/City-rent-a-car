About Us

Welcome to City Rent-A-Car

City Rent-A-Car is a premier car rental company founded with a vision of becoming a leader in “Ground Transportation” for both personal and corporate travel. City Rent-A-Car is backed by a panel of experienced Industry experts who have collective experience of more than 15 years.

City Rent-A-Car offers chauffeur driven car rental services to its customers across all these cities through its wide range of brand new fleet of cars. The fleet of cars includes entry level Economy cars, Multi-utility vans, Sedans and SUVs. It has set up 5 operating locations which are operational 24X7.

In the chauffeur drive segment, City Rent-A-Car offers airport transfers, half day and full day rentals within the city limits, inter-city journeys, monthly hiring with or without chauffeurs and with or without fuel etc. The rental options are wide and can be customized to suit the needs of the travellers. The fleet of cars for Chauffeur drive rentals comprises cars ranging from Mid-size to Super luxury segment manufactured by, Toyota, Honda, Maruti, Hyundai etc. City Rent- A-Car also offers luxury vans and coaches across its network cities on hire. This segment includes vehicles such as VW Crafter, Mercedes Viano, Toyota Commuter, Volvo coaches etc.

City Rent-A-Car also offers weekend chauffeur driven holiday packages to tourist and pilgrimage interested places. This door to door services offers hassle free rentals with 100% reliability on service delivery.
